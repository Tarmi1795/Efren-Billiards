# 📄 Efren Billiards & Events - Site Map

The application consists of a high-performance, single-page architecture using hash-based routing.

## 🏠 Main Entry
1. **Home Page** (`#home` or `/`)
   - **Hero Section**: Dynamic intro with editable CMS content.
   - **Offerings**: Visual gateway to Billiards, Darts, Chess, and Event Place.
   - **Membership Tiers**: Quick view of Bronze, Silver, and Gold benefits.
   - **Facility Tour**: Interactive carousel of the club's premium spaces.
   - **Events & Timetable**: Live schedule of weekly activities.

## 🎮 Game & Service Pages
2. **Billiards Page** (`#billiards`)
   - Legend-focused content with professional table showcase.
   - **Rankings Section**: Live Hall of Fame for localized pool players.
3. **Darts Page** (`#darts`)
   - Professional darts setup details and "Precision & Focus" philosophy.
   - **Rankings Section**: Dedicated dart leaderboards.
4. **Chess Page** (`#chess`)
   - Intellectual sports focus with strategy-themed aesthetic.
   - **Rankings Section**: Elo-based rankings for club members.
5. **Premium Event Place** (`#event-place`)
   - Venue showcase for corporate, birthday, and tournament events.
   - **Budget Estimator**: Real-time calculator for event costs based on guest count and CMS-controlled base prices.
6. **Karaoke & Social** (`#karaoke`)
   - Showcase of private singing rooms and sound systems.
7. **Coffee Menu** (`#coffee-menu`)
   - Digital menu with categories for Espresso Bar, Mocktails, and Snacks.

## 🏆 Competition & Membership
8. **Tournaments** (`#tournaments`)
   - **Live Bracket Visualizer**: Real-time view of tournament progress.
   - **Registration**: Integrated sign-up for upcoming events.
9. **Membership Packages** (`#membership-packages`)
   - Dedicated landing page for tiered membership sales.
   - Feature-by-feature comparison of benefits.

## 🔐 Administrative & Legal
10. **Admin CMS Portal** (`#admin-cms`)
    - Restricted access panel for management.
    - Modules for Hero, Site Images, Food Menus, Tournaments, and Member approvals.
11. **Terms & Conditions** (`#terms`)
    - Legal framework for facility use and membership.
